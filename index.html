about me

Hello! My name is Andrei and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2012 when I was playing around with HTML and CSS for the sake of creating my website where I would review video games.


Fast-forward to today, and I've taken quite the different road a normal front-end developer would choose. After finishing high school, with a specialization in mathematics and computer science, I decided to study law and leave aside anything related to computers or development around this area. But as life happens, after a few events and various cross points I decided it is time to pick up what I left back in 2012 and hopefully make a career out of it, fortunately not reviewing games.


I studied C++ in high school while attending a Javascript optional class. This year I decided to complete a Javascript course with the purpose of expanding my knowledge and skill base, after that I started to learn ReactJs and coming to grow quite fond of it.


Here are a few technologies I’ve been working with recently:

  • Javascript
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • ReactJs
  • Redux
